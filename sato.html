About SATO

SATO is a smart mobile app that transforms your phone into a powerful, real-time learning tool for all things electronics. Just point your camera to scan basic electronic component, and instantly get detailed, easy-to-understand information right at your fingertips. Whether you’re just starting out or already a tech pro, SATO is like having a knowledgeable pocket assistant ready to boost your skills anytime, anywhere. It’s designed to make learning fast, fun, and hassle-free—helping you build confidence and crush your projects without missing a beat.

Features

👁 Instant access to detailed info on electronic components, right when you need it—no waiting, no hassle.
👁 Real-time component detection that lets you scan and identify the compnents on the spot, saving you time and headaches.
👁 Interactive learning features that make understanding components way less boring and actually fun.
👁 Explore our full component library anytime, anywhere — no internet needed, so you’re always powered up.
